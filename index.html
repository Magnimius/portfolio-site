About me

I'm a second year student at UCT, studying my bachelors in Computer Science and Applied Statistics

On top of that, I'm a debating coach for the Western Cape, an editor and TA at Polyphony Lit, and an aspiring writer. I've been published in The Paper Crane Journal, Point Mag, and Polyphony Lit. I've been named one of the top poets of the AVBOB Poetry Competition 2022, and I'm writing my debut novel.

I've coded this site to host my writing, as well as any other coding projects I may do.

My biggest fear is fading into an indifferent middle age. Let this be a testament to whatever I shall do with my life. I hope for it to be meaningful, and impactful.

love, always
